• Bo-Cha Boba Tea

Branding / Packaging / Campaign

Jammed buses, packed elevators, crowded hallways. Working life is all about being close to others (even when you don't want to be). Bo-Cha Boba Tea embodies this philosophy, and brings it to you with a bright fun twist. I paid homage to the cutesy colourful nature of Asian drink packaging. The use of gradients alongside the bold overlapping colours inspired by risograph printing created a blend with the Western aesthetic.

Campaign

The advertising campaign for this product follows the mantra "embrace the squish" and shows how it comes up in the working person's day to day. Utilizing advertisements on places that naturally attract many busybodies and a cheeky message to charm viewers.

The three flavours and illustrations represent various moods people experience daily. The bursting energy in original, monotonous buzz in coffee, and need for relaxation with green tea.
