    • About Me

      Hi there, I’m Caroline, a graphic designer and overall big thinker based in Vancouver. Right now I’m a third year student in the IDEA School of Design with a focus on branding and a smattering of illustration and UX. Research and analysis is where I feel at home—if you’ve got a question or a problem I’m your guy.

      My interests are vast and varied but my one true love is video games. I go from grinding at work to grinding levels virtually and efficiency is my key to success. I live next to computers so feel free to reach out and I’ll get back to you as soon as I can (after one more game).
